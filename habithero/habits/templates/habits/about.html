About HabitHero

Our Mission

HabitHero is designed to help you build better habits by tracking daily tasks, visualizing progress, and staying motivated through streaks and achievement badges.

What We Offer
  • Daily Check-ins: Mark your habits as completed with a simple yes/no toggle.
  • Streak Tracking: Automatically track how many consecutive days you've completed a habit.
  • Visual Calendar: See your habit completion in a beautiful calendar view.
  • Achievement Badges: Earn badges for milestones like 3-day, 7-day, 30-day streaks.
  • Habit Management: Easily add, edit, and delete habits as your goals evolve.
  • Progress Dashboard: Get an overview of all your habits and current progress.
Why HabitHero?

Building habits is challenging, but with the right tools and motivation, anyone can succeed. HabitHero combines gamification with simplicity to make habit tracking enjoyable and effective.
